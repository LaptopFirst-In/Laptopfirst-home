Privacy-First Design & Regulatory Compliance

Operating within India's strict digital legal framework, Mite is engineered from the ground up to adhere to the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023, mitigating litigation risks for institutional financial entities.

DPDP Act 2023 Compliance Architecture
• Explicit Consent Notice: Users or corporate entities sign an explicit, standalone notice during enrollment detailing that the asset is hypothecated/leased and remote lock vectors exist.
• Strict Data Minimization: The background agent never scrapes, reads, or stores personal browser logs, communications, keys, or user data. It strictly monitors system health flags, payment syncs, and telemetry.
• Risk Mitigation Cadence: Tiered notifications prevent accidental loss of business or personal data, shielding lenders from civil litigation over un-backed-up corporate asset wipes.
